About

ESCWA Data Portal site is for open data provided to the public. Rich datasets are available for researchers and government for direct download or automation.

Most dataset pages on this portal only hold metadata for each dataset. The site will have the metadata and links to the data as it exists in those many other locations.

Some datasets are harvested from other UN agencies data archives and other datasets only exist on this portal.
